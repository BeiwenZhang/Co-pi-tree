Benchmark Results

Q1: How effectively does Co-π-tree collaborate with unseen AI partners and real human partners?

Answer: Co-π-tree achieves strong zero-shot collaboration across the five Overcooked-AI layouts, including settings that require role specialization and partner-conditioned task switching.

Co-π-tree is evaluated in Overcooked-AI across five layouts and both role assignments. Baselines include SP, PBT, FCP, MEP, COLE, BC, ProAgent, and CausalPlan.

Human-agent collaboration rewards by layout. The full method obtains the highest mean reward on all five layouts among the full method and ablation variants.

Efficiency

Q2: Can an LLM-generated policy tree reduce online reasoning cost?

Answer: Yes. LLM calls are moved from every decision step to the offline policy-learning loop, so the final tree executes directly during interaction.

Online LLM baselines require repeated model calls during interaction. Co-π-tree uses LLMs during policy construction and refinement; once the final tree is produced, additional evaluations do not require extra LLM queries.

Co-π-tree reduces the number of LLM queries by 77.7% and test-time latency by 97.1% relative to the mean of the online LLM baselines.
